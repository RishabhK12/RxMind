# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/26/2025

RxMind ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy and giving you control over your health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application. By using RxMind, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

## What Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
You may provide photos or PDFs of discharge papers, manually entered medications, schedules, tasks, follow-up appointments, notes, reminders, and questions typed into the app. This information may include health-related data.

### B. No Account Required
You may use RxMind without creating an account. We do not require your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information.

## How Your Data Is Stored

### Local-Only Storage
All data, including medications, tasks, appointments, reminders, notes, and extracted text, is stored locally on your device using secure local storage (such as Flutter Secure Storage for sensitive data and SharedPreferences for non-sensitive app settings). RxMind does not store any user data on external servers.

### Data Never Leaves the Device
RxMind does not transmit your data to third-party services, cloud servers, or external APIs. All processing, organization, and functionality occur entirely on your device.

## How Your Data Is Used

We use your information solely to:
- Extract and organize information from discharge papers  
- Create and manage tasks, medications, and reminders  
- Display summaries, checklists, and recovery progress  
- Support core app functionality and performance  

We do not sell your data, share your information with advertisers, store your data externally, or use your data for marketing or analytics purposes.

## AI and Automated Processing

RxMind may use on-device logic or automated processing to organize and display your information. All processing occurs locally on your device.

RxMind does not provide medical advice, diagnose conditions, or recommend treatments. The app is intended for organizational and informational purposes only.

## HIPAA Considerations

RxMind follows HIPAA-aligned principles such as data minimization, local-only storage, and secure handling of sensitive information. However, RxMind is a prototype, is not a HIPAA-certified platform, and is not a covered entity or a medical provider.

## User Controls

You may delete all app data, remove individual documents or medications, export your data (if enabled), or uninstall the app to remove all local data. RxMind does not retain copies of your health data.

## Children’s Privacy

RxMind is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

## Security Measures

We use secure local storage technologies and device-level protections to safeguard your data. While no system is entirely secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
