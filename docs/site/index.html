RxMind

RxMind is a personal recovery organizer that runs on your phone. Log discharge instructions, organize medications and tasks, set wellness reminders, and export reports — all stored locally on your device.

  • On-device document text extraction
  • Medication and task logging with reminders
  • Neutral lock-screen notifications
  • Encrypted local storage (SQLCipher)
  • Erase all data anytime in Settings
